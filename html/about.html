About me



My name is Aleksa Maletić. I am a 16-year old programmer and tech enthusiast from the Republic of Srpska (Bosnia and Hercegovina). My journey started in the summer of 2016 and since then, I have come to see my career go up. Driven by motivation and hard work, my knowledge expanded like I had never thought.

My programming career is mainly focused on Machine Learning/Artificial Intelligence, but I also have a lot of experience in other fields such as Web Development, Game Development and lately, Cyber Security.

Some of my projects that I've done are on my Github page. Since I am not a big fan of Open-Source, I tend to keep most of my work private. Also, I've made my first desktop app in 2018, which you can download on my Github page.

Over the years, I have gotten many awards for my hard work and dedication. These two are my favorites:
"Branko Ćopić" Primary School - A special award for excellent knowledge in Computer Science, 2020.
"Association of Inventors of Republic of Srpska" - Silver Plaque for Mentorship, 2021.
